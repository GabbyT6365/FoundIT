About FoundIT

FoundIT is a digital lost and found platform designed for students. It provides a centralized system where users can easily report, search, and claim lost items in a structured and organized way. The system allows students to submit detailed reports including item descriptions, locations, and images, making it easier to identify and match lost belongings. This reduces the reliance on manual inquiries and scattered communication across the campus.

On the administrative side, FoundIT enables authorized personnel to review reports and manage claim requests. Admins can verify ownership, approve or reject claims, and maintain the accuracy and integrity of the system. Overall, FoundIT improves efficiency, minimizes lost item incidents, and promotes a more organized and responsive campus environment through the use of a simple and accessible web-based solution.
